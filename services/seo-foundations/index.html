What is in the foundation

Six practical blocks — no mystery package.

Technical baseline

Architecture, Core Web Vitals, crawl/index hygiene, canonicals, mobile performance, robots/sitemap, and schema that matches the real offer.

Answer engine readiness

Clear entities, FAQs, and content shaped so AI Overviews and LLM answers can quote you without inventing a fake business.

Local SEO (Ohio-aware)

Service-area language, listings alignment, proof, and intent pages for the towns and jobs you actually want — not doorway spam.

Content architecture

Service pages, industry wedges, comparison/FAQ depth, and internal links that give topical weight instead of one thin homepage.

Analytics that matter

Launch analytics, conversion events, and source reporting so you can see which pages create inquiries — not vanity bounce metrics alone.

After-launch cadence

New pages, fixes after algorithm shifts, metadata cleanup, and a weekly rhythm when the business is ready to compound.

No. Relaunches, redirects, metadata cleanup, and recovery after messy rebuilds are part of the work when the baseline is broken.

Impulse covers a legitimate starter presence. Resonant SEO foundations are for operators who need structure, local depth, and ongoing compounding — not just a homepage.
