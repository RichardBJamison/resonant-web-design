# Resonant Web Design Landing Top

## Visual Contract

The landing page top follows the provided July 2026 reference: dark technical studio surface, glowing cyan brand system, amber emphasis on the word "pulse", a responsive glass/device composition on the right, a connected capability matrix beneath the primary actions, and a dark cyan service ticker under the hero.

## Tokens

- Background: near-black `#070b13`, deep navy `#0a101a`, glass shadow `rgba(2, 8, 14, 0.72)`.
- Primary glow: cyan `#1ff7f2`.
- Secondary glow: magenta `#f052d8`.
- Warm accent: amber `#ffad4d`.
- Text: white `#f4fbff`, muted blue-gray `rgba(218, 232, 242, 0.74)`.
- Type: `Syne` for logo/headlines, `DM Sans` for readable copy, `JetBrains Mono` for technical labels.

## Layout

- Desktop hero is a wide first-viewport composition rather than a tall photo hero.
- Navigation floats over the dark hero with a neon outline CTA.
- Hero copy sits left-of-center with a single-line headline at large viewports: `Find your pulse.`
- Right art is code-native glass UI: tilted device slab behind a launch-stack proof card.
- Capability cards describe the wider offer: one-on-one marketing design, aggressive SEO, social media integration, AI phone/chat, automations, and business integrations.
- The waveform is ambient and line-based, not a filled illustration.
- The bottom ticker is a dark band with cyan uppercase service labels and dot separators.

## Responsive Rules

- Under tablet width, stack copy above art while preserving the neon proof card.
- Under mobile width, let the headline wrap naturally, keep CTAs full-width, stack capability cards, and scale the device slab behind the proof card.
- Avoid text overlap and keep all fixed-format cards dimensioned with explicit min-heights.
