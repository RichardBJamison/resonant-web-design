FAQ

What services does Resonant offer?

Conversion-focused custom websites, SEO and lead generation, AI phone and lead-response systems, CRM workflows and automation, business-system integrations, hospitality and multi-location ops technology, plus dashboards, APIs, and reporting.

How can I call?

Email me@richardbjamison.com to start. If the project is a fit, we can move to a call and map the fastest useful path.

What sets your web design apart?

The site is built around the business model, the offer, the buyer, the search strategy, and the systems that need to work after launch.

Can you provide examples?

Yes. Current examples include Ohio Valley Land Partners, Open Source Barware, Intelligent Hospitality Systems, DJ Landscaping, and lead-generation demos.

How long does it take?

Focused launch pages can move quickly. Full service websites, SEO systems, and integrations depend on content, scope, and how much operational logic is involved.

What is the cost?

Focused custom website engagements from $999. Search and growth engagements from $699/month. Connected systems are scoped after the business process and technical needs are understood—so the proposal matches the problem, not a fixed package menu.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Ongoing support can include SEO cadence, content updates, analytics, care plans, technical changes, integrations, and campaign tracking.

Can you help with SEO?

Yes. SEO is handled from the first planning pass: technical cleanup, page architecture, local search, structured data, internal links, and content strategy.

Do you offer e-commerce?

Yes. That can include product pages, ordering paths, payment flows, inventory-adjacent workflows, and analytics for what sells.

What industries do you work with?

Current focus areas include restaurants, bars, hospitality operators, landscapers, roofers, land and real estate operators, SaaS tools, and serious service businesses.
